How CostPerDemo Works

1. Define Your Ideal Customer

We start by understanding your target audience and creating a detailed ideal customer profile.

2. Build Your Prospect List

Our team builds a high-quality list of prospects that match your ideal customer profile.

3. Launch Outreach Campaigns

We create and execute personalized outreach campaigns to engage your prospects.

4. Schedule Qualified Meetings

We qualify leads and schedule meetings with prospects who are ready to learn more about your offering.

5. You Close the Deal

You attend the pre-qualified meetings and focus on closing deals. You only pay for the meetings we book.
